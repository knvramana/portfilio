RoboProf - ChatBot using Intelligent Systems

RoboProf is an intelligent chatbot designed to assist students and faculty by answering university-related queries. American Sign Language Alphabet Classification

This project focuses on the classification of the American Sign Language (ASL) alphabet to facilitate better understanding and interaction within the ASL community. Utilizing PyTorch, I developed and trained a deep learning model to classify images into ASL alphabet classes. The goal is to bridge the communication gap between individuals with hearing impairments and those who do not know sign language, thereby making interactions more inclusive.

Graph Coloring Algorithms on Online Graphs

Conducted an empirical study on the competitive ratios of graph coloring algorithms for online graphs at Concordia University. Collaborated with a team to explore the behavior of the First Fit and CBIP Algorithms on various bipartite graphs, focusing on their computational complexity and solution quality. Implemented these algorithms for online graph scenarios and authored an in-depth analysis report. Findings revealed trends such as the First Fit Algorithm resembling an increasing hyperbolic curve, while the competitive ratio in CBIP was independent of the number of vertices.
